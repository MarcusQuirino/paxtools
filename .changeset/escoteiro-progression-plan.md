Add a personal progression plan for escoteiros. Each item (ação fixa, ação variável, especialidade, insígnia, custom action) now has a star to favorite it; the new `/plan` route shows only favorited items in two views — **Por Área** (collapsable per eixo/bloco, same progress bars as the main dashboard) and **Minha Ordem** (a flat, drag-and-drop reorderable list). Pending and completed items automatically sink to the bottom of the ordered list, with pending shown faded and approved-completed struck through.
